Who We Are?

Knit-Tech is a biennial Knitting technology trade fair and has so far completed 16 editions with the 17th edition scheduled for March 01-04,2024. Backed by 27 years of experience it makes us the most knowledgeable about Tirupur's knitting industry scenario. Knit-Tech's reputation as India's leader in showcasing knitting technologies covering the full range from yarn manufacture to the final knitted apparel and finished garments is unchallenged.
